State title

fxStepFreezeSync

Type of sound

Tempo synced Grain Freezer Delay/Reverb
Beware:The feedback range have been deliberately set to allow different kinds of special effects, handle with care as you may get a strong resonant buildup (e.g. with low values for Expression 1).

Controllers

Expression1: Time and pitch random deviation
Expression2: Synced Grain delay time ("pre delay" and "reverb density")
    from zero to 16ths notes (0% to 12.5%)
    8ths triplets (up to 16.6%)
    dotted 16ths (up to 18.75%)
    8ths notes (up to 25%)
    4ths triplets (up to 33.3%)
    dotted 8ths (up to 37.5%)
    4ths (quarter) notes (up to 50%)
    2nd (halfnote) triplets (up to 66.6%)
    dotted 4ths (up to 75%)
    whole note (up to 100%)
Expression3: Grain delay feedback
Expression4: Synced Freeze rate
    16ths notes (0% to 12.5%)
    8ths triplets (up to 16.6%)
    dotted 16ths (up to 18.75%)
    8ths notes (up to 25%)
    4ths triplets (up to 33.3%)
    dotted 8ths (up to 37.5%)
    4ths (quarter) notes (up to 50%)
    2nd (halfnote) triplets (up to 66.6%)
    dotted 4ths (up to 75%)
    whole note (up to 100%)
